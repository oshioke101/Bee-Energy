About Us

Established in 2014, Bee Energy Consultancy Services Limited is a proudly indigenous Nigerian oil and gas consultancy company built on technical excellence, industry experience, and strong operational discipline.

We provide integrated geoscience, engineering, field development, reservoir management, and strategic advisory solutions tailored to the evolving needs of the energy industry. Our expertise spans the full asset lifecycle from exploration and evaluation to production optimization and investment advisory.

With over 200 years of combined industry experience across our leadership and technical teams, Bee Energy has built a reputation for delivering fit-for-purpose solutions that maximize asset value, improve operational performance, and support sustainable energy development.

Driven by professionalism, innovation, reliability, and efficiency, we work closely with operators, investors, and industry partners to execute projects with precision, quality, and long-term value creation.

Our Mission

To deliver reliable, fit-for-purpose geoscience, engineering, and strategic advisory solutions that optimize asset performance, support operational excellence, and create long-term value for our clients and partners.

Our Vision

Too be a leading energy consultancy company recognized for technical excellence, innovation, and sustainable value creation across the energy industry.
